The Game

DWEL is a location-based civilization game that turns the real world into a battleground for territory. Claim hexagonal cells by physically dwelling at locations, build structures, train armies, and defend your domain against other players and roaming barbarian hordes.

Every hex on the map is real ground. Every cell you hold is a place you've stood. Your empire is built with your footsteps.

How It Works

Dwell to Claim

Spend time at a location to spawn units. The longer you stay, the stronger the unit you earn.

Build & Fortify

Construct forts, barracks, watchtowers, armories, and monuments on your territory. Each building grants unique strategic bonuses.

Command Armies

Carry a mobile army with you. Transfer units between garrisons. Deploy strategically to attack or defend.

Fight for Territory

Attack enemy cells and NPC barbarian camps. Combat resolves asynchronously with battle replays.
